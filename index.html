Cardano Mixer

Private Transactions on Cardano


Cardano Mixer breaks the link between the sender and the receiver of assets on Cardano Blockchain, enabling private transactions. This is possible thanks to the usage of zero-knowledge cryptographic proofs. Keep your crypto assets safe and secured.

How does it work?

This is how our mixer protocol works explained in simple terms.

  • To deposit funds, generate a one-time password locally on your machine and send native Cardano assets into the smart contract.

  • Wait some time for other users to interact with the protocol to create uncertainty. Wait longer to receive bigger rewards for anonimity mining.

  • To withdraw funds, generate a cryptographic proof of knowledge of a password for one of the deposits (again, locally) and send this proof to the smart contract.

Q&A

What are the differences between your project and other privacy solutions?

The closest comparison would be Tornado Cash on Ethereum blockchain. While the user experience will be quite similar, there are significant differences including off-chain proof verification and a completely different economic model. Our project aims at becoming the top privacy solution for Cardano blockchain.

Will there be an ICO or ISO?

Most of the tokens will be distributed to the participants of the trusted setup ceremony, early users, and early supporters of the project. More details can be found in our whitepaper.

What about compliance?

The protocol operates in a completely decentralized manner and is country-agnostic. It does not collect any personal information about the users. However, the protocol can generate a report of user's transactions (using local data). These reports allow users to stay compliant with the laws of their jurisdictions.
